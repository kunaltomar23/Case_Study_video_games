# 🎮 Project Overview

The global video game industry generates billions of dollars in revenue every year. Understanding which platforms, genres, publishers, and regions drive sales can help gaming companies make better strategic decisions.

This project performs an in-depth **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on historical video game sales data to uncover market trends, consumer preferences, and factors contributing to successful game launches.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Gaming companies invest heavily in developing and marketing new titles. However, predicting commercial success remains challenging.

This analysis aims to answer key questions:

- 🎮 Which genres generate the highest sales?
- 🕹️ Which gaming platforms dominate the market?
- 🌍 How do sales vary across regions?
- 🏢 Which publishers consistently release top-selling games?
- 📈 Which years witnessed peak gaming industry growth?

The insights can support better business decisions regarding product development, platform selection, and market targeting.

# 📊 Key Insights

### 🎮 Genre Insights

- Action games dominate overall sales.
- Sports and Shooter genres consistently perform well.
- Puzzle and Strategy genres contribute comparatively lower revenue.

### 🕹️ Platform Insights

- PlayStation and Nintendo platforms account for a significant portion of global sales.
- Platform popularity shifts rapidly with new console generations.

### 🏢 Publisher Insights

- Major publishers dominate market share.
- A small group of publishers contributes a large percentage of global revenue.

### 📈 Industry Growth Insights

- The gaming industry experienced rapid growth during the mid-2000s.
- Certain years recorded exceptionally high global sales due to blockbuster releases.

### 🌍 Regional Insights

- North America contributes the largest share of total sales.
- Japan demonstrates distinct genre preferences compared to Western markets.
- Europe remains a major revenue-driving region.
